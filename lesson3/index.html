Site Perpose

The purpose of the Discovery Chamber of Commerce site is to work as a networking platform for business in Discovery and people who work in them or those that are in one way of the other affiliated to those business. Here, they will be able to organise, plan and execute all matters pertaining to common business goals. These goals may include things like how best can such members invest their time, strength and money in developing programs that will improve economic, social and financial wellbeing of the people and businesses in and around Discovery. The chamber will have members who subscribe to it that will be able to select leaders who will set and compile laws and policies for the chamber. These policies will aim to promote and champion the wellbeing of local business in Discovery.
